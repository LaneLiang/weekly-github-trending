Existing DRL methods in PECs are comprehensively reviewed in this article. New findings are identified as follows.
\begin{enumerate}
\item From the application perspective, the DRL methods applied in PECs can be categorized as the design, control, and maintenance. The usage percentage, application trend, features, and requirements of DRL in each life-cycle phase are discussed.
\item From the method perspective, the DRL methods applied in PECs can be categorized as value based methods, policy-based method, actor-critic method and multi-agent DRL. The usage percentage, advantages, and limitations of relevant DRL algorithms in each category are comprehensively compared.
\item The milestones of relevant algorithm and applications are identified and organized as a timeline map.
\item For each life-cycle phase, illustrative examples are discussed and the limits and future research opportunities are identified.
\end{enumerate}
